About me

I'm a full stack developer operating out of Texas. I work on all kinds of sites from all walks of life. From blogs to social media platforms; my skillset allows me to work anywhere.

My favorite type of work is back-end. I love writing servers in either express or with node's http module that allow for easy development. I usualy get sucked into making tools and automating systems so much that I can forget that I also have to do the front-end too.

That doesn't mean that I don't like front-end work, or that I can't make a stellar UI. Colors that mesh and components to match are a staple for all my sites. It's just that making the systems that allow websites to serve unusual content or making charts of data-flow is just something that I am especially fond of.
